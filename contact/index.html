What to Send With Your Request

These details help us understand the work and respond faster.

Photos & Drawings

Send close-up photos, wide photos, drawings, weld symbols, marked-up plans, repair details, sketches, or measurements if available.

Project Location

Include the city, state, jobsite address, facility name, bridge/project name, gate instructions, parking, and site contact if available.

Scope Type

Tell us if the work is structural steel, bridge/DOT, pile splice, commercial, industrial, emergency repair, field fabrication, or subcontract support.

Access Conditions

Include height, lift needs, crane needs, forklift access, barge access, confined access, lane closures, hot work rules, or shutdown windows.

Required Certifications

Let us know if the project requires AWS D1.1, AWS D1.5, DOT requirements, owner requirements, inspection hold points, or certified welder documentation.

Timeline

Include whether the work is emergency, this week, 1–2 weeks out, a bid-stage estimate, shutdown work, or a future project.

Can I request procurement documents through this page?

Yes. Use the form to request COI, W-9, capability information, safety information, UEI, CAGE, eVA ID, SWaM details, SAM.gov information, and certification information.

Yes. We support emergency welding for failed welds, cracked steel, broken brackets, damaged supports, equipment repairs, jobsite rework, and schedule-critical field welding when availability and site conditions allow.

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports qualified contractor scopes across Virginia, Maryland, D.C., North Carolina, South Carolina, and the Mid-Atlantic depending on scope, schedule, travel requirements, and project fit.
