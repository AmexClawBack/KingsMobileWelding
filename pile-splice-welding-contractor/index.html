Pile Splicing Welding FAQ

Do you provide H-pile splicing welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides H-pile splicing support for bridge, railroad, deep foundation, and heavy civil projects depending on project specifications and site requirements.

Do you weld pipe pile?

Yes. We provide pipe pile welding support for marine construction, bridge foundations, docks, piers, bulkheads, cofferdams, and heavy civil projects.

Do you weld sheet pile?

Yes. We provide sheet pile welding support for cofferdams, bulkheads, retaining systems, waterfront structures, attachments, bracing, and field modifications.

Do you weld pile caps, bracing, and pile tips?

Yes. We support pile cap welding, bracing welding, pile tip welding, splice plates, support steel, field modifications, and related welded components tied to pile systems.

Can you cut beams to length, bevel them, and cut out weld-throughs?

Yes. We can cut beams and pile sections to length, bevel ends for weld prep, and cut out weld-through access as required by the splice detail. This can be performed on the jobsite or at the client’s laydown yard.

Can you provide AWS D1.1 or AWS D1.5 certified welding for pile splices?

Yes. Some pile splice scopes require AWS D1.1 structural welding or AWS D1.5 bridge welding certification. Kings Mobile Welding can support certified pile welding scopes when required by the project specifications.

Can you perform CJP or full penetration welds?

Yes. Where required by the project specifications, we support complete joint penetration welds, full penetration welding, proper beveling, root preparation, and backgouging procedures.

What do you need to quote a pile welding project?

Send pile type, weld detail, drawings, project location, estimated quantity, required welding certification, access requirements, schedule, site contact, and any applicable specifications.
