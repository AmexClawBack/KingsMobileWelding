Services We Offer in Gloucester Point

We bring the shop to your site with fully equipped mobile rigs. Certified welders deliver clean fit-up, strong welds and tidy finishes that pass inspection the first time.

  • Structural & Facility Welding: beams, columns, embeds, stairs, rails, platforms (AWS D1.1)
  • Stainless & Aluminum: TIG/MIG for architectural and corrosion-resistant components
  • Pipe & Pressure: carbon/stainless pipe, purge where required, coordination for pressure tests
  • Fabrication & Fit-Up: cut, layout, fixtures, tack & final welds; on-site installation

  • Inspection-Ready QA/QC: WPS/WPQR adherence, COI, capability statement, UEI/CAGE
