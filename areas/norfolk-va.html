Norfolk Jobsite Checklist What we ask upfront

Norfolk work often includes access and coordination constraints. These details help us mobilize fast and avoid surprises.

  • Access & parking:
    Truck access, gates, loading docks, overhead clearance, and staging space.
  • Hot work requirements:
    Permits, spark containment expectations, and site safety rules.
  • Working hours:
    Day/night work windows, noise restrictions, and trade coordination.
  • Lifts & elevation:
    Who provides the lift, working height, and tie-off requirements.
  • Inspection needs:
    Visual only, CWI presence, or documentation required for closeout.
