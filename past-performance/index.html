Industrial Plant Structural & Process Support

We were contracted to support a major industrial facility with the modification of safety railings on a bag palletizer system. This work was essential to allow the plant to upgrade and modernize their equipment. Using carbon steel stick welding with high-tensile strength electrodes, we ensured durable, code-compliant results that met the client’s production and safety standards. Our expertise in structural welding and process support minimized downtime and provided a reliable solution tailored to the plant’s operational needs.

  • Structural steel fit-up and certified welds (AWS D1.1)
  • Equipment supports, platforms, rails, and safety upgrades
  • Coordination with plant safety and inspection teams

Keywords: industrial welding, plant maintenance, structural steel, mobile fabrication

HVAC Contractor Brackets, Stands & Retrofits

Our team partnered with an HVAC contractor to design and fabricate freestanding steel support stands for multiple air conditioning units and condensers. The existing equipment was mounted to a building scheduled for demolition, requiring a safe and stable alternative. We built custom welded steel frames to carry the load independently, ensuring proper airflow, accessibility, and long-term durability. By delivering precision fabrication and on-site installation, we helped the contractor keep critical HVAC systems operational throughout the transition.

  • Custom frames/brackets for air handlers and condensers
  • Roof-safe procedures and corrosion-resistant finishes
  • Rapid response to keep installs on schedule

Keywords: HVAC welding, rooftop stands, custom brackets, fast turnaround

Industrial Contractor — Stair Repairs & Access

We were engaged by a general contractor to perform an urgent repair on a damaged stair at a commercial building. The existing step had rotted out, creating a safety hazard and requiring immediate attention. Our team mobilized quickly, performing on-site demolition of the failed stair component, then fabricated and installed a new steel replacement. Using proven welding and fabrication techniques, we delivered a safe, durable solution under a tight deadline, ensuring the contractor’s project stayed on schedule and the facility remained code-compliant.

  • Stairs, treads, handrails, platforms and catwalks
  • Field fit-up, clean welds, and inspection-ready finish
  • Safety and access improvements with minimal downtime
