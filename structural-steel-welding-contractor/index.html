Structural Steel Welding FAQ

Do you provide AWS D1.1 certified structural steel welders?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 certified structural steel welding support for contractors, fabricators, erectors, and project teams.

Do you perform beam splicing and column welding?

Yes. We support beam splicing, column welding, connection welding, structural repairs, reinforcement, and field-installed steel modifications depending on project specifications.

Can you cut beams to length, bevel them, and cut out weld-throughs?

Yes. We can cut beams to length, bevel ends for weld prep, and cut out weld-through access as required by the detail. This can be performed on the jobsite or at the client’s laydown yard.

Can you perform CJP or full penetration welds?

Yes. Where required by project specifications, we support complete joint penetration welds, full penetration welding, proper beveling, root preparation, and backgouging procedures.

Do you handle field corrections when steel does not fit?

Yes. We provide jobsite welding, cutting, fit-up support, reinforcement, and steel modification support when field conditions require corrections or adjustments.

What do you need to quote a structural steel welding project?

Send drawings, connection details, photos, project location, estimated quantity, required welding certification, access requirements, schedule, site contact, and any applicable specifications.
