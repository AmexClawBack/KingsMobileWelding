What to Send for Fast Steel Pipe, Piling or Casing Welding Pricing

Send These Details

  • Project location, state, site contact, target start date, expected duration, and work hours

Steel Pipe & Piling Supplier Welding FAQ

Do you provide welding support for steel pipe and piling suppliers?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides mobile welding support for steel pipe suppliers, pipe piling suppliers, casing pipe suppliers, pile suppliers, fabrication shops, and their contractor customers.

Can suppliers refer customers to Kings Mobile Welding?

Yes. Suppliers can refer customers to Kings Mobile Welding when supplied pipe, pile, casing, or fabricated steel needs to be spliced, modified, repaired, extended, or welded after delivery.

Do you weld pipe pile splices?

Yes. We support pipe pile splice welding, large-diameter pipe pile welding, steel pipe pile welding, bridge pile welding, marine pile welding, and foundation pipe welding.

Do you weld casing pipe and drilled shaft casing?

Yes. We support casing pipe welding, drilled shaft casing welding, permanent casing, temporary casing, caisson casing, bore casing, utility casing, and field casing splices.

Do you support pile driving and deep foundation contractors?

Yes. We support pile driving contractors, deep foundation contractors, foundation drilling contractors, geotechnical contractors, bridge contractors, railroad bridge contractors, marine contractors, and heavy civil teams.

Can you weld H-pile splices too?

Yes. We also support H-pile splice welding for driven pile foundations, railroad trestles, bridge foundations, pile driving contractors, and heavy civil work.

Do you supply steel pipe or piling?

No. Kings Mobile Welding is a mobile welding contractor, not a steel pipe or piling supplier. We support suppliers and their customers by providing field welding after pipe, pile, casing, or fabricated steel has been supplied to the project.

Do you travel for pipe pile, casing, and supplier-related welding work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding is based in Hampton Roads, Virginia and supports qualified projects across Virginia, Maryland, Washington D.C., North Carolina, South Carolina, the Mid-Atlantic, and travel work for the right contractor scopes.

Do you engineer pipe pile or casing splice details?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding affects structural capacity, pile capacity, casing requirements, bridge components, DOT requirements, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the splice detail or repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, owner, DOT authority, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote supplier-related field welding?

The fastest quote comes from drawings, pipe details, casing details, pile schedules, splice details, photos, project location, access conditions, crane or lift access, pipe OD, wall thickness, steel grade if known, number of welds or splices, inspection requirements, and required start date. Start by sending the scope through our contact page.
