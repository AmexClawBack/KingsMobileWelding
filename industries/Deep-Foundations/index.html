Where Welding Shows Up on Deep Foundation Jobs

Most deep foundation welding is schedule-driven. Contractors usually call when piles must be spliced, casing must be extended, bracing needs to be welded, or field conditions change while the crew, crane, hammer, or barge is already mobilized.

  • Driven piles: pipe pile splices, H-pile splices, pile extensions, alignment, weld-out, and splice plates
  • Drilled shafts: permanent casing splices, casing extensions, large-diameter casing, lifting lugs, and repair welding
  • Sheet pile systems: cap plates, welded attachments, tie-ins, reinforcement, corner details, and field modifications
  • Cofferdams: walers, struts, braces, temporary works steel, penetration reinforcement, and support frames
  • Marine foundations: waterfront pile work, pier foundations, bulkhead-related steel, bracing, and access steel
  • Bridge foundations: DOT pile work, bridge pile splices, pile cap attachments, casing, and foundation-related structural steel

Deep Foundations Welding FAQ

Do you provide pipe pile splice welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports pipe pile splice welding for pile driving contractors, deep foundation contractors, marine contractors, bridge contractors, and infrastructure teams. Send the pile size, wall thickness, splice detail, access notes, and inspection requirements for review.

Do you provide H-pile splice welding?

Yes. We support H-pile splice welding for driven pile, bridge, DOT, marine, and deep foundation projects when the project provides the splice detail, WPS requirements, inspection requirements, and access information.

Can you weld permanent casing or drilled shaft casing?

Yes. We support permanent casing splice welding, drilled shaft casing extensions, large casing joints, fit-up, tack-up, and weld-out support for deep foundation and infrastructure projects.

Are you a PDCA member?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding is a PDCA member and supports pile driving and deep foundation contractors with field welding services tied to driven piles, pipe piles, H-piles, sheet pile, cofferdams, and foundation-related steel.

Do you provide sheet pile and cofferdam welding?

Yes. We support sheet pile and cofferdam welding for walers, struts, braces, cap plates, welded attachments, tie-ins, support frames, and field modifications. Related pages include Sheet Pile Welding and Cofferdam Welding.

Can you mobilize for emergency pile welding?

Yes. We provide schedule-critical field welding support when pile splicing, casing, bracing, or foundation steel must be handled quickly to protect crane time, hammer time, inspection windows, or production schedule.

What areas do you cover for deep foundation welding?

We support deep foundation and pile welding projects across Hampton Roads, Eastern Virginia, Virginia, Maryland, D.C., North Carolina, South Carolina, and the Mid-Atlantic. For qualified contractor scopes, project-based travel is available.

What related service pages should my team review?

Most deep foundation welding scopes overlap with Pile Splice Welding, Cofferdam Welding, Sheet Pile Welding, Marine Construction Welding, DOT & Infrastructure, and Structural Steel Welding.
