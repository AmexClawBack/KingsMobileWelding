Deep Foundations & Pile Driving Contractors

Field welding support for pile driving contractors, deep foundation crews, PDCA contractors, marine foundation teams, and bridge foundation projects.

  • Pipe pile splice welding: large-diameter pipe piles, driven pile extensions, and field splice details
  • H-pile and casing welding: H-pile splices, permanent casing splices, drilled shaft casing, and fit-up support

Marine & Coastal Construction

On-site welding support for marine contractors, pile driving crews, waterfront general contractors, and bridge teams.

  • Cofferdam welding: walers, struts, braces, tie-ins, and temporary works steel

Fabrication Shops

AISC & Structural Steel Fabrication Shops

Welding manpower for AISC fabricators, structural steel shops, and manufacturers that need qualified production support.

  • FCAW / flux core support: production welding for beams, columns, frames, plates, and assemblies
  • CJP and backgouging: carbon arc gouging, prep-heavy welds, and weld sequencing
  • Shop workflow: blueprint reading, weld symbols, fit-up, tolerances, and production pressure
