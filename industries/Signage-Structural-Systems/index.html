Where Welding Shows Up on Billboard & Sign Structure Jobs

Most billboard and sign welding calls are field-driven. Sign installers usually call when steel needs to be fit, repaired, reinforced, or modified during installation.

  • Billboard frames: tube steel, angle frames, crossmembers, gussets, braces, support frames, and face support steel
  • Billboard access steel: catwalks, ladders, platforms, handrails, safety rails, and service access components
  • Pylon signs: poles, base plates, brackets, cabinets, support frames, and sign face mounting steel
  • Monopole and unipole structures: support plates, welded brackets, reinforcement, and field corrections
  • Repair work: cracked welds, corroded steel, damaged frames, loose brackets, and impact-damaged sign structures
  • Installation support: crane-time fixes, fit-up corrections, field modifications, and punch-list welding

Billboard Structure & Sign Installer Welding FAQ

Do you weld billboard structures on site?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides on-site field welding support for billboard structures, steel sign frames, catwalks, ladders, service platforms, support brackets, braces, and repair details.

Do you work with sign companies and billboard installers?

Yes. We work as a welding subcontractor for sign companies, billboard installers, outdoor advertising contractors, general contractors, and steel installers that need certified welding support on active sign projects.

Can you weld pylon signs and commercial sign structures?

Yes. We support pylon signs, freestanding sign structures, sign poles, steel frames, support brackets, base plate details, sign cabinets, mounting frames, and related commercial sign support steel.

Are your welders AWS D1.1 certified?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 structural welding support for sign and billboard projects where qualified welders, proper fit-up, and inspection-ready workmanship are required.

Do you design billboard or sign structures?

No. We provide welding and field fabrication support based on drawings, engineered details, contractor direction, or approved repair scopes. Structural design, wind-load design, foundation design, and engineering should be handled by the appropriate engineer or sign structure designer.

Can you handle emergency sign structure repairs?

Yes. We provide emergency and schedule-critical welding support when cracked welds, damaged frames, loose brackets, impact damage, corroded steel, failed supports, or unsafe access steel create a project delay or safety concern.

What areas do you cover for sign and billboard welding?

We support sign structure and billboard welding projects across Hampton Roads, Eastern Virginia, and the Mid-Atlantic, including Newport News, Hampton, Norfolk, Portsmouth, Chesapeake, Virginia Beach, Suffolk, Williamsburg, and nearby areas.

What related service pages should my team review?
