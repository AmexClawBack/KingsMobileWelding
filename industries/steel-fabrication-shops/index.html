Where Welding Shows Up Inside Fabrication Shops

Fabrication shops usually search for welding help when production is already under pressure. The need is not just a “welder” — it is someone who can step into a shop workflow and help move steel through the building.

  • Structural members: beams, columns, frames, plates, brackets, and shop-built assemblies
  • Connection work: clip angles, base plates, stiffeners, gussets, moment connections, tabs, and welded attachments
  • Production FCAW welding: repetitive structural welds where speed, consistency, and quality all matter
  • CJP and backgouging: prep-heavy welds requiring carbon arc gouging, backgouging, and controlled weld-out
  • Fit-up and tolerance work: alignment, layout, squareness, weld symbols, and practical shop problem solving
  • Material handling coordination: workflow tied to bridge cranes, gantries, forklifts, carts, fitters, and weld stations

Steel Fabrication Shop Welding FAQ

Do you provide AWS D1.1 certified welders for fabrication shops?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports steel fabrication shops and AISC fabricators with AWS D1.1 certified welding support for structural steel shop work, FCAW welding, fit-up support, and production welding needs.

Can you provide FCAW or flux core welders for production work?

Yes. We support production-focused FCAW and dual shield flux core welding for structural steel shops that need consistent output, quality workmanship, and welders who understand shop workflow.

Can you support AISC fabrication shops?

Yes. We support AISC fabricators and structural steel shops that need welding manpower, overflow support, backlog help, or project-based shop welding support. Our related service page is AISC Fabrication Shop Welding Support.

Do you support CJP welds and backgouging?

Yes. We can support prep-heavy structural work involving CJP welds, carbon arc gouging, backgouging, joint prep, and weld-out sequencing when the shop provides the applicable requirements and procedures.

Can you help with temporary or overflow shop welding manpower?

Yes. Many shops use outside welding support when backlog builds, deadlines tighten, overtime increases, or a specific package needs more manpower than the current crew can cover.

Do you work inside the shop or only in the field?

We can support fabrication shop environments as well as field welding environments. For shop work, the scope, process, schedule, safety rules, onboarding, and insurance requirements should be sent before mobilization.

What areas do you cover for steel fabrication shop welding support?

We support shops across Hampton Roads, Eastern Virginia, and surrounding regions, including Newport News, Hampton, Norfolk, Portsmouth, Chesapeake, Virginia Beach, Suffolk, Williamsburg, and project-based work throughout the Mid-Atlantic.
