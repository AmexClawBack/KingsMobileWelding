Where Welding Shows Up on Solar & Utility Jobs

Most utility welding calls are field-driven. Contractors usually call when steel is damaged, a support needs modifying, a racking issue threatens commissioning, or a substation detail needs correction before the next phase can move forward.

  • Substations: equipment stands, cable trench steel, support frames, guards, platforms, brackets, and access steel
  • Solar farms: damaged posts, racking repairs, torque tube repairs, tracker support steel, and bracket corrections
  • BESS sites: container support steel, bollards, equipment pads, skids, guards, and access platforms
  • Utility infrastructure: transformer containment steel, grounding support steel, cable tray supports, and field modifications
  • Commissioning support: emergency welding, punch-list repairs, failed weld correction, and schedule-critical rework
  • Steel erection support: embeds, connection repairs, site steel, frames, and field welding tied to power infrastructure

Solar, Utility & Substation Welding FAQ

Do you provide welding support for substation construction?

Yes. We support utility and substation contractors with structural steel repairs, equipment supports, cable trench steel, access platforms, handrails, brackets, guards, frames, and field modifications tied to substation construction and utility infrastructure.

Do you provide solar farm welding and racking repairs?

Yes. We support solar EPC contractors and utility-scale solar projects with racking repairs, torque tube repairs, tracker support steel repairs, damaged post fixes, bracket repairs, tabs, plates, reinforcement, and commissioning punch-list welding where project specs allow field repair.

Can you support BESS or battery energy storage projects?

Yes. We support BESS and energy storage projects with equipment support steel, container support steel, bollards, guards, brackets, access platforms, inverter skid support, and emergency repair welding tied to project needs.

Are you AWS D1.1 certified for structural field welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 structural field welding support for utility, solar, substation, industrial, and infrastructure projects where qualified welders and inspection-ready work are required.

Can you mobilize for emergency utility welding?

Yes. We provide emergency and schedule-critical field welding when failed welds, damaged steel, racking issues, equipment support problems, or punch-list items threaten commissioning, inspection, outage timing, or production.

Do you work around energization windows and outage schedules?

Yes. We can coordinate around site access, shutdowns, commissioning schedules, energization timelines, outage windows, crane time, and safety requirements when the project team provides those constraints up front.

What areas do you cover for solar and utility welding?

We support solar, utility, and substation welding projects across Hampton Roads, Eastern Virginia, Virginia, the Mid-Atlantic, and surrounding regions. For the right project, we can support qualified contractor scopes outside the local area.

What related service pages should my team review?
