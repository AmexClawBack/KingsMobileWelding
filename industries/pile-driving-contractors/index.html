What to Send for Fast Pile Driving Welding Pricing

The fastest way to get accurate pricing and availability is to send the same information your estimator, superintendent, pile crew, or project manager is already reviewing.

Send These Details

  • Project location, state, site contact, target start date, expected duration, and work hours
  • Drawings, pile schedules, splice details, weld symbols, WPS requirements, repair details, pile cap details, bracing details, or marked-up plans
  • Photos of the pile, splice area, pile cap, bracing, template, work zone, access path, crane setup, or existing field issue
  • Scope type: H-pile splice, pipe pile splice, pile extension, pile cap, bracing, template, cutoff, repair plate, support steel, casing splice, or field modification
  • Material size, H-pile size, pipe OD, wall thickness, steel grade if known, joint configuration, number of splices, and coating requirements
  • Access constraints including crane access, lift access, low-headroom conditions, traffic control, rail access, marine access, shutdown windows, confined work zones, or night work
  • Inspection requirements, DOT requirements, QC/QA notes, project specifications, safety requirements, onboarding requirements, and schedule constraints

Pile Driving Contractor Welding FAQ

Do you provide welding support for pile driving contractors?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides mobile welding support for pile driving contractors, driven pile crews, deep foundation contractors, bridge contractors, railroad bridge contractors, marine contractors, steel pipe suppliers, piling suppliers, and heavy civil teams.

What pile driving welding scopes do you support?

We support H-pile splice welding, pipe pile splice welding, field pile splice welding, pile caps, bracing, templates, cutoffs, repair plates, support steel, casing splices, drilled shaft casing welding, and contractor-directed field modifications.

Do you weld H-pile splices?

Yes. We support H-pile splice welding for driven pile foundations, bridge foundations, railroad trestle work, pile driving contractors, DOT-style projects, and heavy civil field welding scopes.

Do you weld pipe pile splices?

Yes. We support pipe pile splice welding, large-diameter pipe pile welding, casing pipe welding, bridge pile welding, marine pile welding, and foundation pipe welding in the field.

Do you support pile caps, bracing, templates, and repair plates?

Yes. We support contractor-directed welding for pile caps, bracing, templates, cutoffs, repair plates, temporary support steel, field-installed attachments, and related heavy civil support steel.

Do you have railroad trestle or bridge foundation experience?

Yes. Past project experience includes H-pile splicing, pile caps, and bracing on a railroad trestle project. We support railroad bridge contractors, bridge contractors, pile driving contractors, and heavy civil teams that need field welding for pile splices, caps, bracing, support steel, and repair plates.

Do you support casing and drilled shaft casing work too?

Yes. We also support casing pipe welding, drilled shaft casing welding, permanent casing, temporary casing, field casing splices, caisson casing, and deep foundation welding support.

Do you work with steel pipe suppliers and piling suppliers?

Yes. We support steel pipe and piling suppliers, casing pipe suppliers, pipe piling suppliers, and fabrication companies when their customers need H-pile, pipe pile, casing, or related steel sections spliced, modified, repaired, or welded in the field.

Do you travel for pile driving welding work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding is based in Hampton Roads, Virginia and supports qualified projects across Virginia, Maryland, Washington D.C., North Carolina, South Carolina, the Mid-Atlantic, and travel work for the right contractor scopes.

Do you engineer pile splice or pile repair details?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding affects structural capacity, pile capacity, bridge components, DOT requirements, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the splice detail or repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote pile driving contractor welding support?
