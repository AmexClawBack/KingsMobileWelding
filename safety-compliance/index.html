Common Safety & Compliance Items We Support

Every site is different. Before mobilizing, we confirm your requirements so you’re not chasing paperwork or rework mid-stream. When requested, we can support:

  • COI / Insurance verification (general liability and any job-specific requirements you provide)
  • Qualifications & certifications (AWS D1.1 certification documentation as applicable to scope)
  • Onboarding documents (W-9, vendor setup info, company identifiers)
  • JHA / AHA participation aligned to the controlling contractor/facility process
  • Toolbox talks & daily coordination with site safety expectations
  • SDS access for consumables brought to site and housekeeping controls
  • Closeout alignment (photos, punch-list corrections, and documentation handoff as required)

Safe Execution on Active Jobsites

Our workflow is designed to reduce risk and keep your crews moving:

  • Pre-task planning: confirm access, staging, hazards, and sequencing before we strike an arc
  • Area control: barricades, signage, spark containment, and disciplined housekeeping
  • Fall protection coordination: follow site rules and coordinate anchorage/access planning
  • Lift/aerial coordination: work within established access plans and site procedures
  • Occupied / sensitive environments: protect finishes, isolate sparks, and minimize disruption
  • Clear communication: we stay aligned with foremen/supers so the schedule doesn’t drift
