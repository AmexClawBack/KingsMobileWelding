Kings Mobile Welding was built to support the kind of work that happens outside a clean shop environment: field changes, repairs, structural connections, pile splices, bridge-related welding, facility upgrades, industrial repairs, emergency fixes, and schedule-driven subcontract welding. What We Do Now

  • Structural steel welding: beams, columns, embeds, base plates, brackets, stiffeners, platforms, frames, and field connections
  • AWS D1.5 bridge welding support: bridge repairs, expansion joints, remediation work, DOT-related welding, and infrastructure scopes
  • Pile splice and deep foundation welding: H-pile splices, pipe pile splices, permanent casing, sheet pile, cofferdam, and foundation-related support
  • Commercial and industrial welding: facility repairs, equipment supports, platforms, guards, stairs, handrails, bollards, frames, and brackets
  • Emergency welding: failed welds, cracked steel, damaged brackets, broken supports, schedule-critical repairs, and jobsite fixes
  • Welding subcontractor support: certified welding capacity for general contractors, steel erectors, fabrication shops, DOT contractors, and project teams
