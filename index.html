Common Questions

What type of welding contractor is Kings Mobile Welding?

Kings Mobile Welding is a mobile field welding contractor focused on AWS D1.1 structural welding, AWS D1.5 bridge welding, structural steel repair, industrial welding, marine construction welding, fixed ladder installation, commercial welding, emergency welding, pile splice welding, and contractor-directed field fabrication.

Do you provide structural steel welding?

Yes. We provide AWS D1.1 structural steel welding support for beams, columns, joists, embeds, base plates, repair plates, supports, stairs, platforms, handrails, brackets, and field modifications.

Do you support bridge and DOT work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports bridge contractors, DOT contractors, railroad bridge contractors, infrastructure teams, and heavy civil contractors with AWS D1.5 bridge welding capability, AWS D1.1 structural welding, bridge repair support, support steel, repair plates, and pile-related welding.

Do you provide industrial welding?

Yes. We support industrial facilities with plant welding, shutdown welding, maintenance welding, equipment repairs, platforms, brackets, guards, support steel, facility repairs, and emergency repair welding.

Do you support marine construction and waterfront welding?

Yes. We support marine contractors, dock and pier contractors, ports, terminals, marinas, waterfront facilities, and property teams with welding for docks, piers, bulkheads, seawalls, marine pile splices, fender systems, dolphins, access steel, and emergency marine repairs.

Do you install fixed ladders?

Yes. We provide fixed ladder installation support for commercial buildings, roof access ladders, wall-mounted ladders, replacement ladders, access steel, brackets, and property manager or contractor-directed installation scopes. Ladder details should be reviewed against applicable project, owner, OSHA/safety, manufacturer, or engineer requirements when required.

Do you support pile driving and deep foundation contractors?

Yes. We support pile driving contractors, deep foundation contractors, foundation drilling contractors, geotechnical contractors, steel pipe suppliers, casing pipe suppliers, and heavy civil teams with field pile splices, pipe pile splices, H-pile splices, drilled shaft casing, casing pipe, pile caps, bracing, templates, and support steel.

Can you provide COI, W-9, safety information, and certification information?

Yes. For qualified contractor scopes, Kings Mobile Welding can provide COI, W-9, capability details, safety information, certification information, and welder information when required for procurement, onboarding, or project review.

Do you engineer welding details, ladder details, pile splices, bridge repairs, or structural repairs?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding or installation affects structural capacity, pile capacity, casing requirements, bridge components, DOT requirements, ladder compliance, fall protection, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, owner, DOT authority, manufacturer, safety consultant, inspector, or qualified authority.
