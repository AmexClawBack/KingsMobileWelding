Common Questions

What type of welding contractor is Kings Mobile Welding?

Kings Mobile Welding is a mobile field welding contractor focused on AWS D1.1 structural welding, AWS D1.5 bridge welding, pile splice welding, pipe pile welding, H-pile splicing, drilled shaft casing welding, casing pipe welding, heavy civil welding, industrial welding, and contractor-directed field fabrication.

Do you support pile driving and deep foundation contractors?

Yes. We support pile driving contractors, deep foundation contractors, foundation drilling contractors, geotechnical contractors, steel pipe suppliers, casing pipe suppliers, and heavy civil teams with field pile splices, pipe pile splices, H-pile splices, drilled shaft casing, casing pipe, pile caps, bracing, templates, and support steel.

Do you support bridge and DOT work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports bridge contractors, DOT contractors, railroad bridge contractors, infrastructure teams, and heavy civil contractors with AWS D1.5 bridge welding capability, AWS D1.1 structural welding, bridge repair support, expansion joint welding, support steel, repair plates, and pile-related welding.

Can you provide COI, W-9, safety information, and certification information?

Yes. For qualified contractor scopes, Kings Mobile Welding can provide COI, W-9, capability details, safety information, certification information, and welder information when required for procurement, onboarding, or project review.

Do you engineer pile splice, casing, bridge, or structural repair details?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding affects structural capacity, pile capacity, casing requirements, bridge components, DOT requirements, railroad requirements, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, owner, DOT authority, railroad authority, manufacturer, or qualified authority.
