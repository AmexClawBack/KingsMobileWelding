Procurement FAQ

Do you provide a W-9?

Yesour W-9 is provided upon request. Contact us and well send it with any other required forms.

Are you insured and certified?

Yes. We are fully insured and AWS D1.1 certified. We follow WPS/WPQR and prepare inspection-ready welds.

Can you work on short notice for emergency repairs?

Yes. We provide rapid response for outage and emergency repairs with clean, documented closeout.

What documentation can you provide to procurement?

UEI & CAGE, COI, capability statement, SOW alignment, NAICS/NIGP references, and W-9 on request. We can also support site-specific safety/JSA documents.

Do you provide facilities maintenance welding and repairs?

Yes. We handle facilities maintenance welding and on-site repairs, including structural fixes, stainless & aluminum work, pipe & pressure, access/safety upgrades (stairs, rails, platforms), and emergency response. Relevant NAICS: 561210, 561720, 561790. Relevant NIGP: 910-00, 910-63, 910-76.
