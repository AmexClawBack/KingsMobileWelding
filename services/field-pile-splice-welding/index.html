What to Send for Fast Field Pile Splice Welding Pricing

Field pile splice welding scopes price faster when the pile type, casing type, material size, wall thickness, splice detail, access conditions, and inspection requirements are clear up front.

Send These Details

  • Project location, state, site contact, target start date, and expected duration
  • Drawings, pile schedules, casing details, splice details, weld symbols, WPS requirements, or marked-up plans
  • Photos of the pile, casing, pipe, splice area, work zone, access conditions, or existing field issue
  • Pile type: pipe pile, H-pile, casing pipe, drilled shaft casing, caisson casing, foundation pipe, marine pile, bridge pile, or support steel
  • Material size, outside diameter, wall thickness, steel grade if known, joint configuration, and number of splices
  • Access constraints including crane access, lift access, low-headroom conditions, traffic control, rail access, marine access, shutdown windows, or night work
  • Inspection requirements, DOT requirements, QC/QA notes, coating requirements, project specifications, and schedule constraints

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding.
