H-Pile Splice Welding FAQ

Do you provide H-pile splice welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides H-pile splice welding for driven pile foundations, pile driving contractors, railroad bridge contractors, bridge contractors, deep foundation contractors, marine contractors, and heavy civil project teams.

Can you support pile driving contractors?

Yes. We support pile driving contractors with H-pile splices, pipe pile splices, pile caps, bracing, templates, cutoffs, repair plates, temporary support steel, and contractor-directed field welding support. See our pile driving contractor welding support page.

Do you have railroad trestle or bridge foundation experience?

Yes. Past project experience includes H-pile splicing, pile caps, and bracing on a railroad trestle project. We support railroad bridge contractors, bridge contractors, pile driving contractors, and heavy civil teams that need field welding for pile splices, caps, bracing, support steel, and repair plates.

Can you support bridge and DOT-related H-pile welding?

Yes. We support bridge foundation and DOT-related H-pile welding when the project documents, specifications, or responsible party provide the welding requirements. We also support AWS D1.5 bridge welding capability when project requirements call for it.

Do you also support pipe pile and casing splices?

Yes. We also support pipe pile splice welding, field pile splice welding, casing pipe welding, and drilled shaft casing welding.

Do you support pile caps, bracing, templates, and repair plates?

Yes. We support contractor-directed welding for pile caps, bracing, templates, cutoffs, repair plates, temporary support steel, field-installed attachments, and related heavy civil support steel.

Do you work with steel suppliers and piling suppliers?

Yes. We support steel suppliers, piling suppliers, steel pipe suppliers, casing pipe suppliers, and fabrication companies when their customers need H-pile, pipe pile, casing, or related steel sections spliced, modified, repaired, or welded in the field.

Do you engineer H-pile splice details?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding affects structural capacity, pile capacity, bridge components, DOT requirements, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the splice detail or repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote H-pile splice welding?

The fastest quote comes from drawings, pile schedules, splice details, H-pile size, steel grade if known, number of splices, photos, project location, access conditions, crane or lift access, rail or traffic control requirements, inspection requirements, and required start date. Start by sending the scope through our contact page.
