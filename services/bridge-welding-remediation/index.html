Why Bridge Expansion Joint Welding Matters

  • High-wear location: bridge joints take continuous impact from traffic, movement, and weather
  • Water intrusion protection: failed joint welds can contribute to deck deterioration and long-term structural issues
  • Inspection-heavy scope: joint systems are closely monitored and commonly repaired or replaced under DOT programs
  • Recurring opportunity: bridge expansion joint work is a steady source of maintenance and rehabilitation welding demand

Do bridge projects typically require AWS D1.5 certified welders?

Yes. We provide field welding support for H-pile splice welding and related bridge foundation steel where welding is specified. Send the splice detail, material information, location, and schedule through our contact page for pricing and availability.

Yes. We provide field welding support for bridge expansion joint systems, including steel edge rails, armor angles, and related bridge joint repair and replacement work tied to maintenance and rehabilitation scopes.
