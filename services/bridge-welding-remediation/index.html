Why Bridge Expansion Joint Welding Matters

  • High-wear location: bridge joints take continuous impact from traffic, movement, and weather
  • Water intrusion protection: failed joint steel and damaged welds can contribute to deck deterioration and long-term structural issues
  • Inspection-heavy scope: joint systems are closely monitored and commonly repaired or replaced under DOT maintenance and rehabilitation programs
  • Recurring opportunity: bridge expansion joint work is a steady source of maintenance and rehabilitation welding demand
