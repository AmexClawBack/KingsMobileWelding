Bridge Welding & Remediation FAQ

Do bridge projects typically require AWS D1.5?

Many welded bridge scopes reference the AASHTO/AWS D1.5 Bridge Welding Code and DOT-specific requirements. If your plans/specs call out D1.5 (or engineer-approved procedures), we align execution and documentation to the project requirements and inspection expectations.

Can you support pile splice welding (H-pile) on bridge projects?

Yes. We provide field welding support for pile splices and related bridge foundation steel where welding is specified. Send the splice detail, material grade, location, and schedule window through our contact page for fast pricing and availability.

What do you need to quote bridge welding or remediation work?

The fastest quotes come from: (1) drawings/details/spec references, (2) photos of existing conditions (for repairs), (3) location & access constraints (lifts/over-water/work-at-height), (4) traffic control/window requirements, and (5) desired dates. Start on our contact page.

Do you coordinate with inspectors and QC/QA?

Yes. Bridge work is inspection-driven. We coordinate with superintendent/QC and inspection staff for hold points, access readiness, and closeout requirements to reduce rework and maintain schedule.
