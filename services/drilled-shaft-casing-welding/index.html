What to Send for Fast Drilled Shaft Casing Welding Pricing

Drilled shaft casing welding scopes price faster when the casing size, wall thickness, splice detail, access conditions, and inspection requirements are clear up front.

Send These Details

  • Project location, state, site contact, target start date, and expected duration
  • Drawings, casing details, splice details, weld symbols, WPS requirements, drilled shaft details, or marked-up plans
  • Photos of the casing, splice area, work zone, access conditions, or existing field issue
  • Casing type: drilled shaft casing, permanent casing, temporary casing, caisson casing, casing pipe, bore casing, utility casing, or pipe pile
  • Casing outside diameter, wall thickness, steel grade if known, joint configuration, and number of splices
  • Access constraints including crane access, lift access, low-headroom conditions, traffic control, rail access, marine access, shutdown windows, or night work
  • Inspection requirements, DOT requirements, QC/QA notes, coating requirements, project specifications, and schedule constraints

Drilled Shaft Casing Welding FAQ

Do you provide drilled shaft casing welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides drilled shaft casing welding support for foundation drilling contractors, deep foundation contractors, casing pipe suppliers, steel pipe suppliers, pile driving contractors, and heavy civil project teams.

Can you weld permanent casing and temporary casing?

Yes. We support permanent casing welding, temporary casing welding, casing extensions, field casing splices, casing pipe modifications, and contractor-directed casing welding support.

Do you support foundation drilling contractors?

Yes. We support foundation drilling contractors with field casing splices, drilled shaft casing welding, low-headroom casing support, casing extensions, caisson casing welding, and welding around drilling operations.

Do you provide casing pipe welding and pipe pile splice welding?

Yes. We provide casing pipe welding support, pipe pile splice welding, and field pile splice welding for deep foundation, pile driving, bridge, marine, rail, utility, and heavy civil projects.

Can you support low-headroom drilled shaft casing work?

Yes. We can support contractor-directed low-headroom casing work where casing must be installed in shorter sections and spliced in the field due to overhead clearance, equipment limits, or project access conditions.

Do you work with casing pipe suppliers and steel pipe suppliers?

Yes. We support casing pipe suppliers, steel pipe suppliers, pipe piling suppliers, and fabrication companies when their customers need casing, pipe, or pile sections spliced, modified, repaired, or welded in the field.

Do you engineer drilled shaft casing splice details?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When welding affects structural capacity, pile capacity, casing requirements, bridge components, DOT requirements, code-controlled work, life safety, or engineered systems, the splice detail or repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, project specifications, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote drilled shaft casing welding?

The fastest quote comes from drawings, casing details, splice details, casing outside diameter, wall thickness, steel grade if known, number of splices, photos, project location, access conditions, crane or lift access, inspection requirements, and required start date.
