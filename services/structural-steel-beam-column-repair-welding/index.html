Structural Steel Beam & Column Repair Welding FAQ

Do you provide structural steel beam repair welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding support for structural steel beam repairs, W-shape repairs, HSS beam repairs, web reinforcement, flange reinforcement, repair plates, stiffeners, connection repairs, and approved repair details.

Do you provide structural steel column repair welding?

Yes. We support column repair welding for W-shape columns, HSS columns, baseplate areas, cap plates, stiffeners, reinforcement plates, column extensions, and engineer-directed column repair details.

Can you weld HSS beams and HSS columns?

Yes. We support field welding for HSS columns, HSS beams, tube steel repairs, reinforcement plates, cap plates, splice details, connection repairs, and contractor-directed field modifications.

Do beam and column repairs require AWS D1.1 welding?

Many structural steel beam and column repair scopes reference AWS D1.1 along with project-specific requirements. If your drawings, specifications, or repair details call for AWS D1.1 structural welding, Kings Mobile Welding can support the field welding portion of the work.

Can you repair baseplates or column base details?

Yes. We support baseplate welding, column base repair support, stiffener welding, plate washer welding, anchor rod-related steel repairs, and field modifications where welding is shown on approved drawings or directed by the project team.

Do you engineer structural steel beam or column repairs?

No. We perform the welding portion of contractor-directed and engineer-directed structural repairs based on approved drawings, weld symbols, repair details, project specifications, or field instructions. Engineering, design responsibility, and final repair details should come from the project engineer or responsible design professional.

What areas do you serve for beam and column repair welding?

We support qualified structural steel repair welding projects across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington D.C..

What do you need to quote a beam or column repair?

Send drawings, repair details, photos, weld symbols, project location, access notes, inspection requirements, and schedule constraints. Photos of the beam, column, baseplate, crack, failed weld, or repair area are especially helpful.
