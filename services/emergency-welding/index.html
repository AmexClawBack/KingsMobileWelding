How to Get Help Fast

Emergency calls move faster when the key details are clear up front. Send photos, location, and the urgency level so we can quickly determine access, equipment, safety considerations, and availability.

  • Send photos: close-up photos and wide photos showing the work area
  • Send location: address, jobsite name, gate access, parking notes, or GPS pin
  • Explain what broke: failed weld, cracked steel, bracket, support, frame, equipment, rail, platform, or structural component
  • Include access notes: lift needed, forklift access, hot work permit, shutdown window, confined access, or active operations
  • Give your timeline: today, tonight, tomorrow morning, this week, shutdown window, or inspection deadline
