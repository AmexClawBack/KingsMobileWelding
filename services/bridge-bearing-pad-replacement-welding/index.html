Bridge Bearing Pad Replacement Welding FAQ

Do you provide welding support for bridge bearing pad replacement?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding support for bridge bearing pad replacement scopes, including bearing plates, sole plates, masonry plates, girder bearing areas, repair plates, anchor bolt-related steel repairs, and AWS D1.5 bridge welding where required by the project documents.

Do you perform the bridge jacking and full bearing replacement yourself?

Kings Mobile Welding provides the welding support portion of bridge bearing replacement work. Jacking, blocking, bearing design, bearing selection, and final bearing replacement procedures should be directed by the bridge contractor, engineer, DOT owner, or qualified jacking contractor. We can coordinate our welding work around the approved procedure.

What bridge bearing components can you weld?

We support contractor-directed welding for bearing plates, sole plates, masonry plates, repair plates, shim-related steel, girder bearing area details, bearing stiffener areas, and related steel repairs when welding is included in the approved scope.

Do bridge bearing repair projects require AWS D1.5 welders?

Many welded bridge scopes reference the AASHTO/AWS D1.5 Bridge Welding Code along with DOT-specific requirements. If your plans or specifications call for AWS D1.5, AWS certified bridge welders, or qualified bridge welders, Kings Mobile Welding can support the welding portion of the work.

Can you support VDOT and NCDOT bridge bearing work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports qualified bridge, DOT, and infrastructure contractors across Virginia and North Carolina. We also have NCDOT certified welders available for bridge work in North Carolina.

Can you coordinate with inspectors, engineers, and jacking contractors?

Yes. Bridge bearing scopes are commonly inspection-driven and procedure-driven. We coordinate with superintendents, QC, QA, engineers, inspectors, jacking contractors, and project managers around access readiness, hold points, weld sequencing, documentation, and closeout requirements.

What do you need to quote bridge bearing pad replacement welding?

The fastest quote comes from drawings, bearing schedules, repair details, photos, project location, access notes, traffic control or shutdown windows, jacking and blocking notes, inspection requirements, and the required start date. Start on our contact page.
