Bridge Stiffener & Connector Plate Repair Welding FAQ

Do you provide welding support for bridge stiffener plate repairs?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding support for bridge stiffener plate repairs, bearing stiffeners, intermediate stiffeners, transverse stiffeners, repair plates, girder web stiffeners, and AWS D1.5 bridge welding where required by the project documents.

Do you support bridge connector plate replacement?

Yes. We support contractor-directed welding for bridge connector plate replacement, diaphragm connection plates, bracing connection plates, clips, gussets, repair plates, field modifications, and engineered repair details when welding is included in the approved scope.

Do stiffener and connector plate repairs require AWS D1.5 welders?

Many welded bridge repair and rehabilitation scopes reference the AASHTO/AWS D1.5 Bridge Welding Code along with DOT-specific requirements. If your plans or specifications call for AWS D1.5, AWS certified bridge welders, or qualified bridge welders, Kings Mobile Welding can support the welding portion of the work.

Can you support VDOT and NCDOT connector plate repair work?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding supports qualified bridge, DOT, and infrastructure contractors across Virginia and North Carolina. We also have NCDOT certified welders available for bridge work in North Carolina.

Can you weld bearing stiffeners and repair plates near girder bearing areas?

Yes. We can support the welding portion of bearing stiffener repairs, girder bearing area repairs, repair plate installation, and related steel remediation where the work is shown on approved drawings, repair details, or contractor-directed instructions.

Can you coordinate with inspectors, engineers, and steel crews?

Yes. Bridge stiffener and connector plate scopes are commonly inspection-driven. We coordinate with superintendents, QC, QA, engineers, inspectors, steel crews, and project managers around access readiness, hold points, weld sequencing, documentation, and closeout requirements.
