AISC Fabrication Shop Welding Support FAQ

Are your welders AWS D1.1 certified for FCAW processes?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 certified welding support for FCAW dual shield processes, helping structural steel fabrication shops bring in qualified manpower that can get to work quickly.

Do you run 1/16" dual shield with both 75/25 and 100% CO₂?

Yes. We have experience running 1/16" dual shield flux core using both 75/25 mixed gas and 100% CO₂ depending on shop procedures, WPS requirements, and production needs.

Can you perform CJP welds and carbon arc gouging for backgouging?

Yes. We support CJP welding, complete joint penetration welds, carbon arc gouging, backgouging, root cleaning, weld repair prep, and structural joint preparation.

Do you read shop drawings and weld symbols?

Yes. We support fabrication shop work that requires blueprint reading, weld symbol interpretation, layout, fit-up, torch cutting, assembly work, and production welding.

Do you bring your own tools and work day or night shift?

Yes. We bring our own tools and can support day shift or night shift when scheduled in advance. Availability depends on project timing, shop requirements, duration, and location.

Do you have experience operating overhead bridge cranes and gantry cranes?

Yes. We have experience operating overhead bridge cranes and gantry cranes in structural steel fabrication environments for safe and efficient handling, positioning, and production workflow.
