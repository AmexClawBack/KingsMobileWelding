What to Send for Fast Government or DOT Welding Pricing

Public-sector welding pricing moves faster when the scope, specifications, access, certification requirements, and onboarding needs are clear up front.

Send These Details

  • Project location, public agency, prime contractor, site contact, and target start date

Government & DOT Welding FAQ

Do you provide AWS D1.5 bridge welding for DOT-related projects?

Yes. When your project includes bridge components or bridge-adjacent scopes that specify AWS D1.5, Kings Mobile Welding can support those requirements. Many public infrastructure packages also include AWS D1.1 structural scopes, so send the drawings and specifications for review.

Do you provide certified welding for government and municipal projects?

Yes. We provide government, municipal, public works, and DOT welding support, including infrastructure repairs, structural steel welding, pile splice welding, misc metals, and compliance-driven field execution.

Are you SWaM certified and SAM.gov registered?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding is Virginia SWaM certified and SAM.gov registered. Government and prime contractor information includes UEI: ZCY4G7GBGQA3 and CAGE: 11P75.

What documents do you provide for onboarding?

We can provide common contractor documentation such as COI, W-9, capability information, safety information, UEI, CAGE, SWaM details, and welding certification information when required for onboarding, procurement, or closeout.

Can you support VDOT, NCDOT, and regional bridge welding scopes?

Yes. We support bridge and DOT-related welding scopes across Virginia and surrounding states, including AWS D1.5 bridge welding support, bridge repairs, expansion joints, pile splices, and infrastructure field welding.
