What to Send for Fast Steel Deck or Shear Stud Welding Pricing

The fastest way to get accurate pricing is to send the deck or stud information your superintendent, steel erector, deck installer, engineer, inspector, or project manager is already reviewing.

Send These Details

  • Project location, floor/roof level, access notes, and target work date
  • Photos of the deck, beam, girder, weld area, missed welds, damaged deck, opening, or repair area
  • Drawings, deck plans, weld symbols, deck fastening details, shear stud details, RFI responses, engineer notes, or marked-up plans if available
  • Deck type and gauge, weld washer requirements, floor deck or roof deck scope, and whether the work is new construction or repair
  • For shear studs: stud size, stud quantity, base metal, deck condition, access, power/equipment requirements, and inspection requirements
  • Inspection requirements, AWS D1.1 notes, WPS requirements, hot work requirements, lift access, shutdown windows, or active jobsite constraints

Steel Deck & Shear Stud Welding FAQ

Do you provide steel deck welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding support for steel floor deck welding, roof deck welding, weld washers, deck attachments, deck repairs, deck reinforcement, missed weld corrections, and inspection-driven commercial construction welding.

Do you provide shear stud welding support?

Yes. We support shear stud welding scopes after reviewing the project requirements, stud size, base metal, deck condition, access, equipment requirements, inspection requirements, and schedule. Send the stud detail and project notes so we can confirm the setup needed.

Can you weld steel roof deck and floor deck?

Yes. We support both steel roof deck welding and steel floor deck welding when the welding is shown on approved drawings, deck details, specifications, or field instructions from the project team.

Can you help with weld washers and deck attachments?

Yes. We support weld washer welding, deck attachment welding, deck field corrections, deck repair welding, and inspection punch-list work tied to steel deck installation or repair.

Do steel deck welds and shear studs require inspection?

Many commercial construction projects require inspection for steel deck welding, weld washers, shear studs, or structural steel welding. We coordinate with superintendents, QC, inspectors, engineers, and project managers when inspection-ready field welding is required.

Can you repair damaged steel deck?

Yes. We support damaged deck repair welding, field patching, reinforcement around openings, corrective welding, and contractor-directed deck repair details when shown on approved drawings or directed by the project team.

What areas do you serve for steel deck and shear stud welding support?

We support qualified steel deck and shear stud welding scopes across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington D.C..

What do you need to quote steel deck or shear stud welding?

Send drawings, deck plans, deck type, deck gauge, weld washer details, shear stud size and quantity if applicable, photos, project location, access notes, inspection requirements, and schedule constraints.
