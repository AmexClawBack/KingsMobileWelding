Important Note on Anchor Rod Welding

Anchor rods and anchor bolts can be safety-critical. Welding to anchor rods, modifying anchor rods, or adding repair details around column bases should be based on approved drawings, project specifications, an RFI response, or direction from the responsible engineer or qualified authority.

  • We perform contractor-directed and engineer-approved welding work
  • We do not engineer anchor rod repairs, base plate repairs, or structural modifications
  • Send drawings, weld symbols, repair details, photos, and field measurements before scheduling
  • We can coordinate with superintendents, PMs, inspectors, engineers, and steel erectors
  • Documentation support available for COI, W-9, welding qualifications, safety information, and project requirements

What to Send for Fast Plate & Anchor Detail Welding Pricing

The fastest way to get accurate pricing is to send the same information your superintendent, project manager, steel erector, engineer, or inspector is already reviewing.

Send These Details

  • Project location, site contact, access notes, and target work date
  • Photos of the embed plate, base plate, anchor rod, column base, repair plate, support plate, or field condition
  • Drawings, sketches, weld symbols, repair details, RFI response, field measurements, or marked-up photos if available
  • Plate thickness, material type if known, anchor rod size if applicable, and whether the work is inside, outside, elevated, occupied, or schedule-sensitive

Embed Plate, Base Plate & Anchor Rod Welding FAQ

Do you weld embed plates and base plates?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding for embed plates, base plates, bearing plates, support plates, repair plates, and contractor-directed structural steel plate details.

Can you weld anchor rods or anchor bolt details?

We can support contractor-directed and engineer-approved anchor rod or anchor bolt welding details. If welding affects structural capacity, anchorage, base plate performance, or code-controlled work, the detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer or qualified authority.

Are you AWS D1.1 certified for structural steel welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 structural welding support for commercial construction, industrial projects, base plates, embed plates, field modifications, repairs, and structural steel connections.

Can you support column base repairs?

Yes. We support contractor-directed column base repair welding, including base plate details, stiffeners, gussets, repair plates, embed plates, field modifications, and approved repair details.

Do you engineer base plate or anchor rod repairs?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed welding. When work affects structural capacity, life safety, anchorage, column bases, base plates, anchor rods, or code-controlled components, the repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer or qualified authority.

What areas do you serve for embed plate and base plate welding?

We support qualified structural welding projects across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington DC.

What do you need to quote an embed plate, base plate, or anchor rod welding job?

Send drawings, photos, weld symbols, plate sizes, material thickness, anchor rod details if applicable, project location, access notes, inspection requirements, hot work requirements, and the requested work date.

Need Embed Plate, Base Plate, or Anchor Rod Welding Support?

Send drawings, photos, weld symbols, plate sizes, field measurements, anchor details, access notes, and schedule requirements. We will review the scope and respond with availability, pricing direction, and what we need to mobilize.
