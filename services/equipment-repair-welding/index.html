What to Send for Fast Equipment Repair Welding Pricing

The fastest way to get accurate pricing is to send the same information your maintenance manager, facility manager, superintendent, project manager, or contractor is already reviewing.

Send These Details

  • Project location, facility type, access notes, and target repair date
  • Photos of the damaged equipment, cracked weld, frame, bracket, guard, support, rack, cart, hopper, bin, or repair area
  • Material type if known, equipment type, damaged area, and whether the equipment is fixed in place or moveable
  • Drawings, sketches, repair details, manufacturer notes, plant notes, or marked-up photos if available
  • Hot work requirements, safety requirements, lift access, shutdown window, production constraints, inspection requirements, or schedule limitations

Equipment Repair Welding FAQ

Do you provide equipment repair welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides equipment repair welding for damaged frames, broken brackets, guards, supports, racks, carts, hoppers, bins, production-related steel, and contractor-directed repair scopes.

Can you repair industrial equipment on site?

Yes. We mobilize to plants, facilities, warehouses, commercial properties, and jobsites to perform equipment repair welding when the repair can be completed safely in the field.

What kind of equipment can you repair?

We commonly support repair welding for equipment frames, brackets, guards, supports, racks, carts, hoppers, bins, platforms, access steel, facility-use steel, and production-related components.

Can you support shutdown equipment repair work?

Yes. We support equipment repair welding during shutdowns, planned outages, maintenance windows, weekend work, night work, and schedule-critical repair windows.

Can you repair equipment at plants and industrial facilities?

Yes. We support equipment repair welding for plants, industrial facilities, warehouses, commercial construction sites, facility maintenance teams, and contractors.

What areas do you serve for equipment repair welding?

We support qualified equipment repair welding projects across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington DC.

Do you engineer equipment repairs?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed repair welding. When an equipment repair affects structural capacity, safety-critical components, lifting points, pressure components, or engineered systems, the repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, manufacturer, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote an equipment repair welding job?

Send photos, project location, equipment type, material type if known, damaged area, access notes, repair description, shutdown window, hot work requirements, and any drawings or sketches available.
