What to Send for Fast Commercial Field Welding Pricing

The fastest way to get accurate pricing is to send the same information your superintendent, project manager, facility manager, or contractor is already reviewing.

Send These Details

  • Project location, jobsite type, access notes, and target work date
  • Photos of the steel, support, bracket, pipe support, platform, guard, access steel, field condition, or repair area
  • Drawings, sketches, weld symbols, repair details, RFI response, field measurements, or marked-up photos if available
  • Material type if known, repair location, and whether the work is inside, outside, elevated, occupied, or schedule-sensitive
  • Hot work requirements, safety requirements, lift access, inspection requirements, shutdown window, and schedule limitations

Field Welding Services for Commercial Construction FAQ

Do you provide field welding services for commercial construction?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides field welding services for commercial construction projects, general contractors, steel erectors, renovation contractors, industrial contractors, facility project teams, and active jobsites.

Can you perform commercial construction welding on site?

Yes. We mobilize to commercial construction sites, industrial jobsites, facilities, warehouses, and active project locations to perform field welding when the work can be completed safely on site.

What kind of commercial field welding can you do?

We commonly support field welding for brackets, supports, platforms, mezzanines, stairs, ladders, rails, guards, pipe supports, equipment supports, repair plates, failed welds, structural steel, and contractor-directed field modifications.

Can you support steel erectors and general contractors?

Yes. We support steel erectors, general contractors, industrial contractors, renovation contractors, mechanical contractors, facility managers, and project teams that need welding support on active jobsites.

Can you support punch-list or schedule-critical field welding?

Yes. We support field welding for punch-list work, fast-turnaround repairs, night work, weekend work, shutdown windows, and schedule-critical commercial construction scopes.

What areas do you serve for commercial field welding?

We support qualified commercial field welding projects across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington DC.

Do you engineer commercial field welding repairs?

No. Kings Mobile Welding performs contractor-directed and owner-directed field welding. When a repair or modification affects structural capacity, life safety, engineered systems, equipment integrity, or code-controlled components, the repair detail should be reviewed by the responsible engineer, manufacturer, or qualified authority.

What do you need to quote a commercial field welding job?

Send photos, project location, drawings or sketches, repair details, weld symbols if available, access notes, lift requirements, hot work requirements, inspection requirements, schedule constraints, and the requested welding scope.

Need Field Welding for a Commercial Construction Project?

Send photos, project location, drawings, access notes, repair description, inspection requirements, and schedule constraints. We will review the scope and respond with availability, pricing direction, and what we need to mobilize.
