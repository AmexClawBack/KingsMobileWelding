What to Send for Fast Heavy Civil Welding Pricing

Heavy civil welding scopes price faster when the drawings, weld details, project requirements, site access, inspection requirements, and schedule are clear up front.

Send These Details

  • Project location, state, site contact, target start date, expected duration, and work hours
  • Drawings, weld symbols, repair details, pile schedules, casing details, structural details, WPS requirements, or marked-up plans
  • Photos of the work area, existing condition, material, access path, staging area, and field issue
  • Scope type: pile splice, casing splice, bridge welding, structural repair, support steel, bracing, pile cap, repair plate, platform, frame, bracket, equipment support, or emergency repair
  • Material size, wall thickness, steel grade if known, joint configuration, number of welds, number of splices, and coating requirements
  • Access constraints including crane access, lift access, low-headroom conditions, traffic control, rail access, marine access, shutdown windows, confined work zones, or night work
  • Inspection requirements, DOT requirements, QC/QA notes, project specifications, safety requirements, onboarding requirements, and schedule constraints
