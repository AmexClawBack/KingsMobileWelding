Field-Welded Structural Steel Connections & Splices FAQ

Do you provide field-welded structural steel connection welding?

Yes. Kings Mobile Welding provides AWS D1.1 field welding support for structural steel connections, welded plates, brackets, clip angles, shear tabs, gussets, stiffeners, field corrections, and construction site welding.

Can you weld beam splices and column splices?

Yes. We support beam splice welding, column splice welding, HSS splice details, W-shape splice details, splice plates, cap plates, stiffeners, and field-installed splice details when shown on approved drawings or directed by the project team.

Can you help with steel erection field corrections?

Yes. We support missed welds, fit-up corrections, field conflicts, shop-to-field issues, mislocated steel support, connection corrections, punch-list welding, and inspection-driven rework.

Do structural steel connections and splices require AWS D1.1 welding?

Many structural steel connection and splice welding scopes reference AWS D1.1 along with project-specific requirements. If your drawings, specifications, or repair details call for AWS D1.1 structural welding, Kings Mobile Welding can support the field welding portion of the work.

Can you weld clip angles, shear tabs, brackets, and plates?

Yes. We support welding for clip angles, shear tabs, brackets, gussets, stiffeners, connection plates, splice plates, reinforcement plates, field-installed plates, and contractor-directed steel attachments.

Do you engineer structural steel connections or splice repairs?

No. We perform the welding portion of contractor-directed and engineer-directed structural welding based on approved drawings, weld symbols, splice details, repair details, project specifications, or field instructions. Engineering, design responsibility, and final repair details should come from the project engineer or responsible design professional.

What areas do you serve for field-welded connections and splices?

We support qualified structural steel connection and splice welding projects across Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Maryland, and Washington D.C..

What do you need to quote connection or splice welding?

Send drawings, connection details, splice details, weld symbols, photos, project location, access notes, inspection requirements, and schedule constraints. Photos of the splice, connection, fit-up issue, missed weld, field conflict, or repair area are especially helpful.

Need Field-Welded Connection or Splice Welding?

Send drawings, splice details, connection details, photos, project location, access notes, inspection requirements, and schedule constraints. We will review the scope and respond with availability, pricing direction, and what we need to mobilize.
