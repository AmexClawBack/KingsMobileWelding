Meet the Owner

I’m Dillon King, owner-operator of Kings Mobile Welding. I bring 9 years of hands-on welding experience and over a decade in the construction industry to every project—so you get work that’s practical, buildable, and aligned to how jobsites actually run.

My background includes field and shop work across oil & gas, manufacturing, industrial facilities, and heavy structural scopes. I’ve welded and supported projects in Texas, Oklahoma, Missouri, Montana, and Virginia.
