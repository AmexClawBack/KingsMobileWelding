What is Pile Splice Welding?

Pile splice welding is the process of joining two sections of steel piling—typically pipe piles or H-piles—to achieve the required depth or load-bearing capacity.

This work is commonly required on:

  • Bridge foundations (DOT projects)
  • Marine construction (bulkheads, piers, docks)
  • Deep foundation systems for commercial construction
  • Driven pile systems requiring field extensions

Proper fit-up, bevel preparation, and welding technique are critical to ensure structural performance and pass inspection. Most projects require compliance with AWS D1.1 Structural Welding Code or AWS D1.5 Bridge Welding Code.
